About me

Hello, I am Jasmin. I am an artist from Switzerland but I currently live and study in Ireland. I'm a freelance artist creating character designs, animations and video games!

I study "Interactive digital art and design" which is a long title to say that I am learning how to combine art, design and programming! I do a lot of drawing, animating, webdesign, UI/UX design, video game design and programming with c++ and html. It's a great study path for me as I really enjoy both aspects, the technical and the creative and using my skills in both to create stuff.

When I am not working on the computer, I like to bake, play tabletop rolepalying games or craft my own charms and earrings from clay. I love cats and the theme of this whole website was inspired by my little mascot, the Apricat!
